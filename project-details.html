Philadelphia Seventh-day Adventist Church Website

Project Overview

Developed a modern, responsive website for Philadelphia Seventh-day Adventist Church to enhance their digital presence and community engagement. The platform serves as a central hub for worship services, events, and member resources.

Key Features

  • Live streaming integration for worship services
  • Event calendar and registration system
  • Member portal with secure login
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • SEO optimization for local search
  • Content management system for easy updates

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Integrating live streaming while maintaining fast load times

Solution: Implemented lazy loading and optimized video embedding with fallback options

Challenge: Making the site accessible to all age groups

Solution: Used large fonts, high contrast colors, and intuitive navigation patterns

Project Details
  • Client: Philadelphia SDA Church
  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Status: Live & Maintained
  • Team Size: Solo Developer
